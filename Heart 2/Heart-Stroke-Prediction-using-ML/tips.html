Tips to keep your heart healthy ❤️

Short, practical daily habits you can adopt to support cardiovascular health.

Daily tips

Eat healthy food

Favor fruits, vegetables, whole grains and lean proteins; limit saturated fats.

Exercise daily

Aim for 30 minutes of brisk walking or a mix of cardio and strength most days.

Avoid junk food

Reduce fried, processed items and sugary drinks; choose whole foods instead.

Limit salt & sugar

Use herbs for flavor and check labels to reduce hidden sodium and added sugars.

Maintain healthy weight

Balance calories and portion sizes; small, consistent changes add up.

Get 7–8 hours sleep

Keep a regular sleep schedule—good rest supports heart and metabolic health.

Manage stress

Try short daily practices like deep breathing, meditation, or gentle yoga.

Avoid smoking & alcohol

Quit smoking and limit alcohol; both lower cardiovascular risk significantly.

Drink enough water

Stay hydrated—aim for about 1.5–2 L/day, more if you are active.

Regular checkups

Monitor blood pressure, cholesterol and blood sugar; act early on abnormalities.
