Project purpose

Problem Statement

Heart disease is one of the leading causes of death worldwide, and India is no exception. Despite how common the problem is, many cases go undetected until it's too late for quick treatment. The main reason is that most people don't seek medical help until they already feel symptoms, and by then serious damage may have occurred.

Traditional diagnosis requires lab tests, ECGs, and visits to specialists. All of this takes time and money. For patients in rural areas, it's even harder. There aren't enough hospitals or specialists, and many people don't know much about heart health. Critical warning signs often go unnoticed for years.

Right now, there's no simple tool that lets regular people check their own heart risk without going to a clinic. This gap in preventive healthcare, especially in rural areas, is the main problem that NeuroCardiaX aims to solve.

Proposed Solution

NeuroCardiaX is a web-based AI system that predicts heart disease risk using machine learning. It's built to be accessible to anyone with a smartphone or computer, without requiring medical knowledge. Users enter a few health details and get an instant risk assessment labeled as Low, Medium, or High, along with a simple explanation.

How It Works

NeuroCardiaX uses a straightforward approach. The system was built using data from over 2,000 patient health records. These records were cleaned and used to train a Random Forest model, which is effective at identifying heart disease patterns.

Key Features

  • Self-Check Mode: Quick heart risk estimate using basic health metrics.
  • Advanced Clinical Mode: Detailed clinical inputs for precise predictions.
  • Instant Results: Risk score within seconds of form submission.
  • Mobile-Friendly: Works on smartphones for rural accessibility.

Technology Used

The project uses Python with Pandas, NumPy, and Scikit-learn for machine learning. Flask serves the model as a REST API, and React.js builds the responsive user interface.

Benefits and Impact

The biggest benefit is speed and accessibility. Users get instant risk assessments without waiting weeks for medical appointments, making early detection possible for rural communities.

Next steps to productionize

  • Compute and report validation metrics (accuracy, ROC AUC) on holdout data.
  • Expose a secure backend endpoint that loads the trained model for predictions.
  • Perform external validation and clinical review before any deployment.
